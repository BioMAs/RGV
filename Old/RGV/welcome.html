Galaxy quick use guide

To enhance your experience, we implemented a Galaxy bioinformatics workflow environment in order to host genomics tools essential for data comparisons between genome releases, and above all between species
Galaxy is an open source, web-based platform for data intensive biomedical research. If you are new to Galaxy start here or consult our help resources.
