Get involved

You want to share your dataset with the reproductive science community and to make it available in the ReproGenomics Viewer database, please feel free to contact us!
For RNA or DNA sequencing data the easiest thing to do is to first upload your data in the Gene Expression Omnibus (GEO) and to the Sequence Read Archive (SRA). Then send us an email with the corresponding GEO and SRA identifiers (and, if available, the PubMed identifier) and we will do our best to integrate your data in RGV.
